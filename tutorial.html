Creating a new program

  1. Open +5 and go to the "Programs" tab.
  2. Click the "+" symbol at the top-right of the screen
  3. On the "Add Program" screen, enter a name for your program
  4. Under "PROGRAM ELEMENTS", click "+ Element" to add a single element (or "+ Combo" to create a combination)
    • If adding a single element, select an element and click "Add __ to program".
    • If adding a combo, click "+ Select Element" and select the first element. Click "+ Add to combo or sequence" to repeatedly add elements to the combo. Once complete, click "Add combo/sequence to program"
  5. Repeat the above to continue adding elements and/or combos to your program
  6. Once complete, click "Save"

Logging a runthrough

  1. After you create a program, go to the "Log" tab on the bottom
  2. Click the "+" symbol at the top-right of the screen
  3. Select your program (may be done already by default) and optionally add a note.
  4. Click "Next".
  5. At the top of the list, you will see a new runthrough. Click on it to log your runthrough.
  6. For each element in your program, tap to toggle through outcomse
  7. Once complete, click "Save" at the bottom of the screen.
